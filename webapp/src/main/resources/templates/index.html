Exchange

Exchange books, get points, spend points to discount books. If you’re downsizing, spring cleaning, or helping family or friends clean house, bring in the books you are ready to part with. When you bring in books, we count the number of paperbacks, hardcovers and mass markets and, according to their condition, give you points for them. The points live on our system for a long time. Exchange points give you discounts on books that you purchase at our store. Moving out of the area? That’s fine, you can donate our points to teachers, who always need help buying books for their students.
